# Vispeak v0.1.1 (First Public Release)

Vispeak is a local, offline voice dictation application for Windows that allows you to instantly transcribe speech into text anywhere. Simply press the global hotkey to start recording, speak naturally, and the transcribed text will be automatically pasted into your active text field.

## Key Features
- **Global Hotkey:** Trigger dictation seamlessly using `Ctrl+Space` (default), supporting both toggle and push-to-talk modes.
- **100% Local Processing:** Your voice never leaves your machine, ensuring complete privacy and fast transcription.
- **Multiple Models:** Choose from various models for transcription (Whisper, Parakeet, Canary, GigaAM for Russian, Nemotron, Qwen).
- **Customizable Overlay:** Three overlay skins to match your preference while dictating.
- **Dictation History:** Built-in history to review and manage previous transcriptions.
- **Flexible Pasting Methods:** 5 different ways to paste text into your target application.
- **Auto-Updates:** Seamlessly receive the latest features and bug fixes.

## Privacy
Vispeak operates entirely offline. There is absolutely no telemetry, cloud API integration, or data collection. Your voice and transcribed text remain strictly on your local device.

## Installation
1. Download the installer (`Vispeak_0.1.1_x64-setup.exe` or similar) from the Assets section below.
2. Run the installer. *Note: Since this release does not have a paid signing certificate, Windows SmartScreen may show a warning. Click **"More info"** and then **"Run anyway"** to proceed.*

## Requirements
- Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit)
